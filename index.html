Machine learning, in plain language

From "what even is a model?" to reading real papers — one interactive page at a time. Every idea starts with everyday words; the math and jargon arrive only after you already understand the thing they name.

Welcome back
How this site works Each page: an everyday explanation, a hands-on demo you can poke at, optional math you can unfold layer by layer (every symbol is named and clickable), the real code, and a short quiz. Use ← → arrow keys to move between pages.
