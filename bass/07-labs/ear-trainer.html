How to get good at this

  • Start with the checked subset (thirds, fourth, fifth, octave) — add intervals only when you're above ~80%.
  • Replays are free. Listen as many times as you want; guessing fast teaches guessing.
  • Sing before you answer. Matching the notes with your voice, even badly, is what wires them in — the song-anchor table helps.
  • Descending is its own skill. Most people learn ascending first and are surprised; train both.
