How to use it

  • Start on frets 0–5, "name" mode. That region holds most of early bass life; get it under 2 seconds per note.
  • "Find" mode is the inverse skill — it's what you use when reading charts. Both directions matter.
  • Think landmarks, not counting: the dots, the 5th-fret rule, octave shapes. If you're counting from the nut, slow down and re-anchor.
  • Wrong answers feed your flashcards — the notes that fool you will keep visiting until they don't.
