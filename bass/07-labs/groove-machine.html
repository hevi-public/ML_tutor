How to practice with it

  • Match the cues first — play exactly what the bass cues play until the pattern is in your hands, then turn "bass cues" off and own it alone.
  • Roots-only is a complete workout: any progression, roots on beat 1, until the changes stop surprising you.
  • Steal the styles: each Unit 6 page deep-links here with its groove pre-configured.
  • Gap training: turn pads AND cues off — just clicks and the highlighted chart. You are the harmony now.
